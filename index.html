About Us

Founded in 2025, Zakhira Rewards is an innovative, incentive-based, multi-level affiliate marketing platform designed to reward customers for their purchases at our exclusive Zakhira Store, both online and offline. We specialize in offering a curated selection of luxury products, including apparel, watches, bags, shoes, sunglasses, and accessories—ensuring that our customers enjoy the finest quality at affordable prices. At Zakhira, we believe in empowering our community. With over 1,000 resellers and marketers already making substantial income through our successful business model, we provide unmatched opportunities to thrive. Our strategic marketing tools, along with a user-friendly website, make it simple for anyone to become part of our journey. Whether you're looking to shop premium products or start your own entrepreneurial venture, Zakhira Rewards allows you to do so without any upfront investment. All it takes is a mobile phone and the drive to succeed. Join us today and unlock the potential to become a successful entrepreneur while enjoying the luxury products you love.

Types of Income

Earn ₹25 cashback on every product purchased at Zakhira Store, online or offline. This reward can be redeemed on your next purchase or withdrawn to your bank account after 24 hours.

Earn ₹25 for every product purchased by your direct referrals. For example, if 10 referrals buy 10 products each, you’ll earn ₹2,500 (100 products x ₹25). The more your network shops, the more you earn!

You earn ₹25 for each product purchased by users referred by your direct referrals, up to 7 levels:

Example:

Level 3: 100 users x 10 products = ₹25,000

Level 4: 1,000 users x 10 products = ₹250,000

Level 5: 10,000 users x 10 products = ₹2,500,000

Level 6: 100,000 users x 10 products = ₹25,000,000

Level 7: 1,000,000 users x 10 products = ₹250,000,000

Total Generation Income: ₹277,775,000

As your network grows, your Generation Income increases!

For every purchase in the Watches & Bags category, both the buyer and their direct sponsor earn a bonus income of ₹100 each! This bonus is our way of giving back to our loyal customers and helping them earn more with every purchase.

All active users (those who've bought at least 1 product) are eligible for our Schemes. To qualify, just introduce a set number of new active users (only direct Level 1 users). Achieve this, and unlock extra rewards or prizes as a thank-you for helping us grow!
