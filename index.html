Cashback Income

With every purchase at Zakhira Store, buyers are guaranteed a cashback of ₹25 on each product, whether purchased online or offline. This ensures that you earn value with every transaction, making shopping with us even more rewarding.

Note: All cashback rewards can be redeemed on your next purchase or withdrawn to your bank account after 24 hours.

Referral Income

At Zakhira Store, you earn ₹25 for each product purchased by your direct referrals. For example, if you have 10 direct referrals and each of them buys 10 products (such as t-shirts or any other items), you will earn ₹25 for each product purchased by your referrals.

So, if each of your 10 direct referrals buys 10 products, that’s a total of 100 products purchased (10 referrals x 10 products). You will earn ₹25 for each of these 100 products, which means you will receive ₹2,500 in referral income (100 products x ₹25).

This system allows you to earn rewards based on the purchases made by your network, increasing your income as your referrals shop!

Generation Income

Generation Income is the earnings you make from the purchases of users referred by your direct referrals, extending up to 7 levels. You earn ₹25 for each product purchased by users in Levels 3 to 7.

Example:

Level 3: 100 users buy 10 products each = ₹25 * 100 users * 10 products = ₹25,000

Level 4: 1,000 users buy 10 products each = ₹25 * 1,000 users * 10 products = ₹250,000

Level 5: 10,000 users buy 10 products each = ₹25 * 10,000 users * 10 products = ₹2,500,000

Level 6: 100,000 users buy 10 products each = ₹25 * 100,000 users * 10 products = ₹25,000,000

Level 7: 1,000,000 users buy 10 products each = ₹25 * 1,000,000 users * 10 products = ₹250,000,000

Total Generation Income:

₹25,000 + ₹250,000 + ₹2,500,000 + ₹25,000,000 + ₹250,000,000 = ₹277,775,000

As your network expands, your Generation Income grows, with each level bringing more earning opportunities from the purchases of users within your network.

To participate, active members need to introduce a certain number of new active users (only direct users on level 1 are counted) as part of the scheme's achievement criteria. Upon successfully meeting the requirements, users will be rewarded with exciting extra benefits or prizes.

These schemes are designed to encourage growth and provide additional rewards for your efforts, making it even more rewarding to be part of the Zakhira Store community.
